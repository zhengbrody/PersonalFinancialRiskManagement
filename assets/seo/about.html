MindMarket AI

Mind Market portfolio risk analytics for individual investors.

MindMarket AI, sometimes searched as Mind Market, helps investors analyze portfolio risk, drawdown, VaR, stress scenarios, market context, ticker research, and AI-generated risk summaries.

The platform combines quantitative portfolio analytics with server-managed AI analysis. Users can create their own portfolio, import holdings from CSV, run risk analysis, and review market or ticker research in one dashboard.

Core features

  • Portfolio risk dashboard with VaR, CVaR, drawdown, stress testing, and factor exposure.
  • AI analyst summaries for portfolio risk, market context, and ticker research.
  • User-owned portfolios stored with authentication and row-level access controls.
  • Market intelligence from public and premium data sources, with clear data-source status.

Important disclaimer

MindMarket AI is for educational and research use only. It is not financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. AI-generated analysis can be incomplete or incorrect, and market data may be delayed or unavailable.
